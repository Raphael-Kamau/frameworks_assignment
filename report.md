# Analysis Report

## Summary
We explored the CORD-19 metadata to understand publication trends, journal activity, and title patterns.

## Key Findings
- Most papers were published in 2020 and 2021.
- Top journals include medRxiv and bioRxiv.
- Frequent title words include "COVID", "SARS", and "pandemic".

## Challenges
- Large dataset required sampling.
- Many missing values in journal and abstract fields.
- Streamlit layout needed careful filtering to stay responsive.

## Learnings
- Gained confidence in data cleaning and visualization.
- Learned how to build interactive dashboards with Streamlit.
- Understood the importance of preprocessing for clarity.
